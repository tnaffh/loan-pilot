Our story

Built to serve the under-served

Established in 2019, Raccoons Financial Services embarked on a transformative journey in the Namibian financial landscape. We fully commenced operations on 2 October 2023 — only once we had the right tools, strategies and people in place to serve our clients well.

Raccoons was driven by a clear observation: a significant gap existed in how financial services were offered to individuals in Namibia. Traditional methods often left borrowers feeling overwhelmed and underserved. We aimed to change that.

Under the supervision of NAMFISA, our team of financial experts is dedicated to redefining the lending process — flexible, tailored, and focused on each client's individual needs.

Raccoons Financial Services is a registered microlender in the Republic of Namibia and is regulated by the Namibia Financial Institutions Supervisory Authority (NAMFISA), NAMFISA licence no. 25/11/1471. Lending is subject to status, affordability assessment and our terms and conditions — after all deductions, clients retain at least 50% of gross pay. Interest rates and fees are levied in accordance with the Microlending Act and NAMFISA determinations. Borrowing more than you can afford to repay can lead to financial difficulty — please borrow responsibly.
